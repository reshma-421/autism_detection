Our Services

Parent Coaching

When your child’s been diagnosed with autism, it may seem overwhelming. You may even feel powerless in your ability to help your child. Know this is not the case. Hedgehog Health can help. Through Parent Coaching, we help you better understand behaviors, modify responses, and reinforce your child’s ABA program at home. It’s a service that empowers and strengthens children, parents, and the entire family.

The Hedgehog Health Parent Coaching Process.

step one
Contact Us. A member of our Care Team will reach out within 24 hours.
step two
Find Your Coach. After a brief interview, you will be partnered with the care team member who will be your primary contact, coach, and support.
step three
Get Insurance Details. We’ll contact your insurance to determine coverage and possible out-of-pocket expenses, and eliminate surprises.
step four
We meet with Your Board-Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) to discuss goals and develop a coaching plan.
step five
Find Your Coach. After a brief interview, you will be partnered with the care team member who will be your primary contact, coach, and support.

Our Parent Coaches
and Program

All of our Parent Coaches are BCBAs with a minimum of a master’s degree and extensive experience in implementing ABA programs, as well as parent training.

Lessons Can Include:

Replacing meltdowns with communication.

Play based activities to teach eye contact, imitation, and communication

Daily living skill learning activities.

Parents and Parent Coaches. A Proven, Powerful Combination.

In 2005, Swallows and Graupner conducted a 4-year longitudinal study comparing Parent Coaching with the typical, center-based treatment model. The study consisted of 24 participants, 12 in each group. There were no significant differences between the groups at pre-testing. Interestingly, the groups were similar at post-testing, too. In fact, half of the kids in each group were succeeding in regular education classrooms at age seven.
