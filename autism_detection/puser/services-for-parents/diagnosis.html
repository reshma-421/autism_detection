Diagnosis

When your child’s been diagnosed with autism, it may seem overwhelming. You may even feel powerless in your ability to help your child. Know this is not the case. Hedgehog Health can help. Through Parent Coaching, we help you better understand behaviors, modify responses, and reinforce your child’s ABA program at home. It’s a service that empowers and strengthens children, parents, and the entire family.

The Hedgehog Health Parent Coaching Process.

step one
Contact Us. A member of our Care Team will reach out within 24 hours.
step two
Find Your Coach. After a brief interview, you will be partnered with the care team member who will be your primary contact, coach, and support.
step three
Get Insurance Details. We’ll contact your insurance to determine coverage and possible out-of-pocket expenses, and eliminate surprises.
step four
We meet with Your Board-Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) to discuss goals and develop a coaching plan.
step five
Find Your Coach. After a brief interview, you will be partnered with the care team member who will be your primary contact, coach, and support.

Early Signs that Your Child Should be Assessed

While every child is different, there are some common behaviors that may prompt you to have your child pre-screened for autism. The following behaviors can be seen throughout your child’s first 15-24 months.

Preference for alone time over playing with other children
Absence of smiles or avoidance of eye contact
Sensory issues to lights, sounds, smells, textures, and tastes
Lack of communication skills
Negative behaviors sparked by changes in routine

Our People and Process Make The Difference.

An autism diagnostician needs to be a doctor who specializes in the mind and its development. Ours are psychologists, developmental pediatricians, neurologists, and psychiatrists. They have all the credentials, experience, and compassion you and your child need.  Here’s the big difference: We don’t restrict ourselves to a specific geographical area when sourcing diagnosticians.
